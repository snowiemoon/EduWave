EduWave Features

Our study organizer offers a range of tools to help you succeed.

Note Reminders Time-Table Calendar Timer Goals

Notes

Take multiple notes and follow up your school lectures better than ever before!

  • Give heading and start writing notes
  • Change custom colored notes for fun appearence
  • Create new note
  • Edit & Delete notes as your command
  • Search your notes by typing into the search-bar

Reminders

Set reminders for assignments and never forget to finish them before the due anymore!

  • Add reminder title & description
  • Select date & time for reminding you
  • Schedule Reminder
  • Remind you in specified date & time

Time-Table

Create time-tables to keep your day organized and showcase your professionalism!

  • Add time for the time-table
  • Add your own timetable
  • Edit or Delete routines
  • Add routines for all the days for that specified time

Calendar

Mark your important dates and track your progress for a outstanding results!

  • Select a date
  • Enjoy your calendar all year!
  • Add events for important dates

Timer

Set timer when practicing exercises to keep you focuson your journey of improvements!

  • Specify Hour, Minute, Second for time for the timer
  • Stop or Reset the timer
  • Set timer to start the countdown
  • Study & Chill along with the timer

Goals

Determind academic goals for motivation and track your accomplishments!

  • Add goals as many as you wish
  • Accomplish them
